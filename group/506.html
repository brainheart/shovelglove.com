trying them out...
I have no clue how big or little you are and this is really 
something very individual...I use my hands and arms alot in my work 
as I am a massage therapist (you should see the look on my clients 
faces when I tell them I exercise with a sledgehammer at home! 
Usually I assure them it won't be part of their treatment!) If you 
feel, when you pick up an eight pounder, that it's not too easy, 
then get that one... Be prepared for the first one or two workouts 
to be a bit hard.. it will get much more comfortable fairly quickly, 
but just don't do what I did the first time I SG'd, which was to put 
on LedZepII with a 12 lb one and go ballistic! LOL... Only go half 
ballistic at the beginning...there's no need for stupidity here (I
