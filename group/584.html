that ankle twist... So I took the last minute of SG to use the 
weight to apply pressure to the quads and all around the knee cap...
This is a fantastic way to self massage those quads... just gently 
roll the head back and forth perpendicular to the muscles fiber 
direction (quads go up and down) and "voila" you are giving yourself 

cross fiber friction massage!
