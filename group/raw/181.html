Oh, yes, you bet I've ridden the injury train! It was a hip flexor/SIJ issue two years back(working with a pitchfork, no less!). It took an embarrassingly long time to fix, after which, to deadlift 225 was all I could do. Live and learn. Lots of trips to the chiro, some massage, ice, stretching of different sorts, and I learned a lot about body awareness, repair, form and tension. The SG was respected and today, I am not sore (I have a very physically active life), save for a bit in the paws and midback.
