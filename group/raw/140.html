Hi Kai,

I'm glad to hear you're finally noticing it in your
abs. I think the trouble is your other parts get so
slaughtered in the beginning that you don't notice
right away.

I too notice and enjoy the "get out of bed NOW" side
effect. I think if it were a longer stretch of time
you might be tempted to say "forget it, it's too late"
and roll back over asleep. But 14 minutes extra sleep
just ain't worth it. Shovelglove gives you the nudge
to wake up without the excuse of its being too much.

I'm glad you find the phases helpful. Unfortunately,
it's not merely "progressive." I just hadn't thought
of some of the moves till later. Some of them might
have been great to start with.

I have fond memories of phase 4. I was there for a
long time. It's tricky to fit in the extra moves,
timewise, of 5a until you're very practiced. But just
cut some of the reps of the other moves and you should
be OK.

Keep on doing what you're doing, taking it slow.
There's no rush. The magic 3 weeks is right around the
corner.
