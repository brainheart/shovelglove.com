This is how I did it pre-baby. It's a little more
complicated now, but that's usually what I try to do.
Because of baby duties, some days I have to do it
after dinner (post on this subject pending). I
wouldn't obsess over the timing: delegate that to your
daily self. Your daily self has this job to do: 14
minutes of shovelglove, and should be able to figure
that out without micromanagement. 14 minutes is short
enough that you can remember right before bed and
still squeeze it in. No make ups, no excuses. If you
miss a day, you wince, and try not to miss the next
one, that's all. That being said, it's good to have a
*default* time at least...
