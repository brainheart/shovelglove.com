How to Make and Use a Shovelglove

Take a sledgehammer and wrap an old sweater around it. This is your "shovelglove." Every weekday morning, set a timer for 14 minutes. Use the shovelglove to perform shoveling, butter churning, and other movements inspired by traditional manual labor until the timer goes off. Stop. Rest on weekends and holidays.
